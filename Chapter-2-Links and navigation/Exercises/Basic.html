Jab We Met

A journey of love, laughter, and finding oneself

Overview

Jab We Met is a romantic comedy-drama that follows Aditya, a heartbroken businessman, and Geet, a lively and talkative Punjabi girl, who meet on a train and end up on an unexpected journey that changes both their lives. Through twists, turns, and hilarious detours, they discover love, identity, and new beginnings.

Key Information

  • Genre: Romantic Comedy
  • Directed by: Imtiaz Ali
  • Released: October 26, 2007
  • Language: Hindi

Cast & Creative Team

Starring Kareena Kapoor as Geet Dhillon and Shahid Kapoor as Aditya Kashyap. Directed and written by Imtiaz Ali. Produced by Shree Ashtavinayak Cine Vision Ltd. Music by Pritam Chakraborty.

Main Characters

  • Geet Dhillon – A spirited and fearless girl from Bhatinda, full of life and dreams.
  • Aditya Kashyap – A reserved and serious businessman going through emotional turmoil.
