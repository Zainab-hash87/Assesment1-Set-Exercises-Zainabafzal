Plot

"Jab We Met" follows the journey of Aditya, a disillusioned businessman, and Geet, a lively and talkative Punjabi girl. Their chance encounter on a train changes both of their lives. As they travel across India, the film explores themes of love, self-discovery, and finding joy in unexpected places.
