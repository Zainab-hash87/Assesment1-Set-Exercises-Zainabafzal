Quick Overview

Honey Garlic Chicken is an epic 12-minute recipe with a magic 5-ingredient sauce. Perfect for busy nights!

This recipe works for any protein: beef, pork, tofu, shrimp, etc.

Prep Time: 4 mins | Cook Time: 8 mins | Total: 12 mins

Servings: 2 | Calories: 400

  • 500g chicken breast
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1/4 cup flour
  • 3.5 tbsp unsalted butter
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1.5 tbsp apple cider vinegar
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1/3 cup honey
