Honey Garlic Chicken Breast

Quick Overview

Honey Garlic Chicken is an epic 12-minute recipe with a magic 5-ingredient sauce. Perfect for busy nights!

This recipe works for any protein: beef, pork, tofu, shrimp, etc.

It’s a great way to use up leftover chicken, too!

Prep Time: 4 mins | Cook Time: 8 mins | Total: 12 mins

What You Need

These are the ingredients you need to make Honey Garlic Chicken:

  • 500g chicken breast
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1/4 cup flour
  • 3.5 tbsp unsalted butter
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1.5 tbsp apple cider vinegar
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1/3 cup honey

How to Make

  • Halve a chicken breast so that two breasts provide four pieces.
  • Applying flour on the chicken helps the sauce stick to it better by giving it a tiny crust.
  • Add butter, garlic, vinegar, soy sauce, honey, and seared chicken.
  • The sauce should thicken after simmering.
  • Coat the chicken in the delicious honey garlic sauce by turning it.
  • And that is it!
