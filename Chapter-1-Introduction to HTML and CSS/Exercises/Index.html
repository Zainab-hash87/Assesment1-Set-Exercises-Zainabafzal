"Quick Overview"

Honey Garlic Chicken is an epic 12-minute recipe with a magic 5-ingredient sauce. Perfect for busy nights!

>

Prep Time: 4 mins | Cook Time: 8 mins | Total: 12 mins

Servings: 2 | Calories: 400

"What You Need"

>
  • 500g chicken breast
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1/4 cup flour
  • 3.5 tbsp unsalted butter
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1.5 tbsp apple cider vinegar
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1/3 cup honey

"Methods for Preparation"

  • Halve chicken breast into thin pieces.
  • Coat lightly with flour for crispy crust.
  • Pan-fry and add butter, garlic, vinegar, soy sauce & honey.
  • Let sauce thicken and coat the chicken well.
  • Done in 12 minutes!

"What to Serve With It"

Great with rice, veggies, cauliflower rice or even mac & cheese!

Try it with cucumber salad or fresh parsley-lemon cauliflower rice.
