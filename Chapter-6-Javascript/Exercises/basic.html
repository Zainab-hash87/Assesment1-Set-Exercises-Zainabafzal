What You Need

These are the ingredients you need to make Honey Garlic Chicken:

  • 500g chicken breast
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1/4 cup flour
  • 3.5 tbsp unsalted butter
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1.5 tbsp apple cider vinegar
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1/3 cup honey

Methods for Preparation

  • Halve a chicken breast so that two breasts provide four pieces.
  • Applying flour on the chicken helps the sauce stick to it better by giving it a tiny crust.
  • Add butter, garlic, vinegar, soy sauce, honey, and seared chicken.
  • The sauce should thicken after simmering.
  • Coat the chicken in the delicious honey garlic sauce by turning it.
  • And that is it!

And THAT's IT!

What to Serve with Honey Garlic Chicken Breast

It comes with enough sauce to absorb into rice or whatever you are serving it with, as well as to drizzle over vegetables. A little goes a long way because the flavor is powerful enough that you do not need a lot!

It is seen above with cucumber salad on the side and cauliflower rice mixed with lemon juice, parsley, salt, and pepper.

Despite having soy sauce in the Honey Garlic Sauce, this dish does not taste particularly Asian. However, it is so adaptable that it will pair equally well with fried rice, macaroni salad, or even mac & cheese.

Yes, indeed! You will understand when you give it a try.
